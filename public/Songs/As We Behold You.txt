As we behold You, as we behold You,
We are changing into Your image.
As we behold You, as we behold You,
We are changing from glory to glory.
#2
As we behold You in all of Your glory,
Lord, by Your Spirit we are changing
Into Your image from glory to glory,
As we behold You, living God.
