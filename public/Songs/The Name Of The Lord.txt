#C
For You alone are God, and I bow before You.
You alone are God, I worship, adore You.
You alone are God, none other before You,
And I offer up my life again.
#1
The name of the Lord is a strong tower;
The name of the Lord brings refuge and strength.
The name of the Lord gives hope to the hopeless;
The name of the Lord breathes life to the dead.
#2
The name of the Lord give strength to the weary,
The name of the Lord brings freedom from fear;
The name of the Lord gives peace to the restless,
The name of the Lord will heal the oppressed.
#3
O Lord, You never change,
Holy God, You remain the same,
For Your love, it never fades,
Your faithfulness surrounds me.
#4
The name of the Lord covers me with mercy,
The name of the Lord brings everlasting joy;
The name of the Lord will lift all myburdens,
The name of the Lord, it makes mecomplete.
#5
I call, and You answer me,
For You know my every need.
In Your love I put my trust,
Your faithfulness surrounds me.
#6
O taste and see
That the Lord is good.
How blessèd is the man
Who hides himself in Him.
#A
Louise Hunt & Nathan Fellingham
