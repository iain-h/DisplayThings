#1
Holy, Holy, Holy is the Lord God almighty.
Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty.
All the angels cry out holy;
All the angels exalt Your name,
Crying holy, holy, holy,
Holy is the Lord.
#2
Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty.
Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty.
All Your people cry out holy;
All Your people exalt Your name,
Crying holy, holy, holy,
Holy is the Lord.
#3
Glory, glory, glory to the Lord God Almighty.
Glory, glory, glory to the Lord God Almighty.
The whole earth is filled with Your glory;
The whole earth will exalt Your name,
Crying holy, holy, holy,
Holy is the Lord.
#A
Andy Park
