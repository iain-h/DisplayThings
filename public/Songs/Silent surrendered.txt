Silent, surrendered, calm and still,
Open to the word of God.
Heart humbled to His will,
Offered is the servant of God.
#2
Come, Holy Spirit, calm and still,
Teach us, heal us, give us life.
Come, Lord, O let our hearts
Flow with love and all that is true.
