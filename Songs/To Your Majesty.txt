#1
To Your majesty,
And Your beauty I surrender.
To Your holiness,
And Your love I surrender.
For You are an awesome God who is mighty,
You deserve my deepest praise;
With all of my heart,
With all of my life I surrender.
#A
Sue Rinaldi & Steve Bassett
