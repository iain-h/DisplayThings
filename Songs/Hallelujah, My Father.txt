Hallelujah, my Father,
For giving us Your Son;
Sending Him into the world,
To be given up for men.
Knowing we would bruise Him
And smite Him from the earth.
Hallelujah, my Father,
In His death is my birth;
Hallelujah, my Father,
In His life is my life.
