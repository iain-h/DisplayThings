#1
Closer to You, Lord, and closer still,
Till I am wholly in Your will.
Closer to hear Your beating heart,
And understand what You impart.
O Breath of Life, come purify
This heart of mine and satisfy.
My deep desire is to worship You,
Lord of my life, come closer still.
#A
Patricia Morgan
